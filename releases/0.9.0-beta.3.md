# Viandly 0.9.0-beta.3

Released: July 21, 2026

This closed-beta update strengthens baking workflows and makes Viandly's more complex features
easier to discover without leaving the current task.

## Highlights

- Added explicit recipe-component links for poolish, levain, sponge, preferment, and other prepared
  ingredient sections, including measured and entire-component usage.
- Added recursive overall baker's formulas that expand linked components into their underlying
  ingredients while preserving each authored section-local formula.
- Expanded the maintained baking-weight catalog across common flours, liquids, dairy, sweeteners,
  fats, salts, leaveners, grains, seeds, fruit, chocolate, and related baking ingredients.
- Carried component relationships through AI import, AI repair, recipe development, editing,
  account archives, print, Cooking Mode, public recipes, and private recipe sharing.
- Added contextual Help for cookbook building, scanner imports, and ingredient conversions, with
  full task-oriented articles available in the public Help Center.
- Expanded baker's-percentage guidance with practical explanations of flour basis, hydration,
  scaling, product profiles, conversion overrides, and preferment formulas.
- Improved Help search behavior and touch-friendly field clearing.

## Compatibility

This release adds nullable ingredient-component relationships. Existing recipes and ingredient
data remain unchanged until a user or AI-assisted workflow explicitly links one section as a
component of another.
